About Us

Discover the history, mission, and people creating the Society of Psychological Counselors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to integrate the professional environment of psychological counselors, promote cooperation between specialists, and support educational processes and improve the professional skills of our members. We strive to create a place that enables the exchange of experiences, professional development, and an innovative approach to counseling work.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in expanding knowledge and skills primarily in the field of psychological counseling. We provide a space for exchanging valuable insights and innovative working methods that improve the quality of services offered by our members. We want every counselor in our community to be able to reach their full professional potential.

History of TDP

The Society of Psychological Counselors was founded as a legal organization in 2019 by a group of enthusiasts and professionals. TDP took its final shape in 2024 through a complete transformation, rebranding, development of a new direction, and establishing a lasting partnership with the Psychopedagogical Society and the Hobweek group. Since then, TDP has been dynamically developing, attracting more and more members who work together to raise professional standards in Poland.
