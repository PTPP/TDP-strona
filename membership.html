Psychological Counselor Levels

Minimum requirements for each membership level.

Counselor Level I

  • Completion of at least a study program in psychology, pedagogy, or related fields.
  • Documented practical internship supplemented by training courses.

Counselor Level II

  • Minimum Bachelor's degree in social sciences or humanities.
  • Documented practical internship.

Counselor Level III

  • Minimum Master's degree in social sciences or humanities.
  • Documented practical internship.

Counselor Level IV

  • Minimum Doctorate degree in social sciences or humanities and documented practical internship.
  • Alternatively: minimum Master's degree with conducted research and published scientific publications.

Application and Promotion Procedure

New Members

  1. Fill out the online form at certyfikacja.psychcon.org.
  2. Submit required documents (diplomas, certificates).
  3. Pay membership fee after positive verification.
  4. Receive certificate and panel access.

Promotion and Specializations

To obtain a higher level or specialization:

  1. Log in to your Counselor Panel.
  2. Submit an application for level upgrade or specialization addition.
  3. Submit documents confirming fulfillment of requirements (according to checklist).
  4. Membership and Certification Committee will verify your application.
